Where to get an idea?

You can get ideas from:

  • Like minded people like you guys
  • By joining online communities in G+
  • Random “legit” websites based on your interest
  • MOOCs or Massive Open Online Course like edX, Coursera, Udacity and many others
  • Friends/families
  • Look out of the window [of a house or car] observe
  • Daydream

Notes:

friends/families = you can get an idea simply by having a conversation or discussions with your friends and families. Have a social life, not an online social life but a “real” social life. Like this meetup we are having right now. No programmer must be deprived of their own social life.
